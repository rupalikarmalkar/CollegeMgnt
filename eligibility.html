SCOEM BTech Admission 2022 :

Details around COEP BTech admission 2022 are as follows:

  • BTech (MHT CET) Eligibility:
    Candidates must have passed Class 12 or equivalent with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
    along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ CS/ IT /Agriculture/ Engineering Graphics/ Business Studies/
    Technical Vocational subject OR a must have passed a Diploma in Engineering and Technology with
    at least 45% aggregate in either of the two (40% aggregate for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Selection:MHT CET/ JEE Main
